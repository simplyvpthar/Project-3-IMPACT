IMPACT

At IMPACT, we understand that the communities where we operate are far more than just the physical locations of our facilities. These communities are made up of our families, coworkers and neighbors. They are also the places where our employees choose to live and raise their families, and where our future workforce is educated and trained. IMPACT is committed to supporting our employees, their family members and the people of communities.

This program focuses on making a positive difference through our contributions, deeper connections, and close relationships with organizations that make our society better. The program will encompass all IMPACT employees, geographies and will include options for employees to contribute in addition to the IMPACT annual contribution. Together, making an IMPACT.

Charity brought to you by IMPACT

  • Partner with a charitable organization globally and provide an annual donation as IMPACT.

  • Focus of the charity should aim at improving educational opportunities in some way.

  • The charity selected should focus on assistance to families of color and assist low income/resource students and/or communities.

Scholarship brought to you by IMPACT

  • 3 Students sponsored annually.

  • Grand(children) of current employees in relevant countries.

  • Recent high school graduates or current undergraduate colleges students Studying business or engineering major.

  • Up to 4 consecutive year award if certain criteria continue to be met.

Pure Funding brought to you by IMPACT

  • Internal fund set up to assist employees in times of need.

  • Employee submit individual requests for themselves or someone else; OR several employees receive assistance (tornado, other natural disaster, etc.).

  • IMPACT guarantees a minimum balance for the fund; otherwise it is funded by employee contributions.
